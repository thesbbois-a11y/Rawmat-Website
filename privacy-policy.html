1. Introduction

Rawmat Coffee Company Limited ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA) of Thailand.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you use our services.

2. Scope of Services

This policy applies to all services provided by the Company, including:

  • RAWMAT mobile application
  • In-store self-service kiosks
  • Delivery platforms (e.g. Grab)
  • Website and related services

3. Personal Data We Collect

3.1 Identity Information

  • Name

3.2 Contact Information

  • Email address
  • Phone number

3.3 Transaction Information

  • Order history
  • Purchase details
  • Loyalty points and rewards

3.4 Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Application usage data

3.5 Location Data

Approximate location for store selection or delivery.

4. Sources of Personal Data

  • Direct user input (registration, orders)

  • Third-party platforms (e.g. delivery partners)

5. Purpose of Data Processing

  • To provide and operate our services
  • To process orders and transactions
  • To manage user accounts
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve products, services, and operations
  • To personalize user experience and promotions
  • To communicate updates, offers, and notifications
  • To comply with legal obligations

6. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contractual necessity

We may share your personal data with:

  • Service providers (e.g. payment processors, cloud systems)

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
