Our Story

RAWMAT Coffee Thailand is a modern coffee brand focused on delivering a seamless, high-quality coffee experience through both physical and digital channels.

We operate an integrated ecosystem including mobile application ordering, in-store self-service kiosks, and delivery platforms. Our operations are fully cashless to ensure speed, accuracy, and convenience.

Our goal is to provide consistent product quality while leveraging technology and data to enhance customer experience and operational efficiency.
